Project Details
Our web application is designed to provide you with essential data about 250 countries, making it easy to explore key details such as capital cities, population, area, and continents.
Whether you're a curious traveler, a passionate researcher, or an international business professional, Capital Data provides you with an immersive experience to deepen your understanding of countries, even those lesser-known locations that you may have never heard of before.
With our user-friendly form, you can easily craft activities related to a maximum of 8 countries. Select the activity name, set the difficulty level, and choose the season it takes place in. Whether it's extreme activities like hiking or surfing, or more traditional options like going for a walk or experiencing local cuisine, the Activity Creator allows you to tailor activities that resonate with your interests and preferences.
